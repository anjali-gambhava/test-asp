Webcast - Test System
This page will help you determine if your computer and Internet connection will allow you to enjoy the webcast without interruption. The test page is divided into three sections.

The first section "Here are the system requirements for this webcast" gives a general description of the hardware and software your computer is required to have installed.

The second section "Here are your system results" involves running a few automated tests of your system. If you see a green "pass" then you're all set. If a red "fail" appears then your computer is missing a required element. You will then see suggestions on how to fix the problem.

The third and final section is "Test your computer by selecting the test webcast below". If you see the video and slides change you're good to go.
